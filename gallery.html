Izuku Midoriya

Izuku is the main protagonist of the My Hero Academia manga and anime series. Even though Izuku was born with no special power, he want to be a great hero who has an innate heroism and a strong sense of justice like the legendary hero All Might.

Kitagawa Marin

Marin is the main female protagonist in the Sono Bisque Doll wa Koi wo suru series. One day, the male protagonist Wakana, was planning to use the school's sewing machine to work on a doll but coincidentally, Marin comes in and spots him. Marin asks if Wakana could help her create cosplays, which Wakana accepts. Thus, a friendship blossoms between the two and the series begins.

Nilou

Nilou is a playable character in Genshin Impact. A dancer under the Zubayr Theater, Nilou's dancing is elegant and graceful. However, outside the stage's spotlight, she is just like any girl her age; warm, smiling, and friendly.
